LJARI App

LJARI App

LJARI project is for the students and teachers of standard 8 to 12. LJARI was founded by internationally known alcohol researchers who recognized the advantages of alcohol delivery in the form of vapors, but who wanted to develop a system that was environmentally safe, compact, easy to use, and most importantly, a system that could lead to precisely targeted blood alcohol levels (BALs) in various animal models.There are two tablets used in the experiments, One is attached with the chamber where the input of the drugs would be done and in the second tablet the results will be there which can be seen by various students.

My contribution to this is implement Face Recoganization using aws SDK and add drug effect on face various points.Bug fixing of various screens in app.

Complexity Crunch

Solutions for time and space complexity based data structure problems.
